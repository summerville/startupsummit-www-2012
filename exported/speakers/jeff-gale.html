Jeff Gale Founder, TicketBiscuit

Jeff has been in the web experience business since 1997, when he co-founded Nylon Designs in Brooklyn, NY. Jeff developed the original TicketBiscuit system in 2001, and in 2005, turned his full attention to the ticketing needs of the entertainment industry.

Jeff earned his BA in Drama and Theatre Arts cum laude from Columbia University in 1996. He is a self-taught engineer and businessman.
